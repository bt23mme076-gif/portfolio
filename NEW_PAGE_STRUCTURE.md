## 🎯 Key Improvements

### 1. **Benefit-First Messaging**
- Hero: "I automate what's eating your time" (not "I build tools")
- About: "I build systems that save you money" (not "Engineer. Full-time builder")
- Every section leads with client benefit

### 2. **ROI-Focused Case Studies**
- Reordered by impact (22% NDR drop first)
- Every case shows measurable results
- Tags include ROI metrics ($400/mo saved, 8hrs/week)

### 3. **Clear Pricing (NEW!)**
- 3 service packages with USD pricing
- Fixed-scope projects (no hourly rates)
- Clear deliverables and timelines
- Starting prices: $1,150 - $2,000

### 4. **Trust Signals (NEW!)**
- US-friendly (timezone, payments)
- Fast turnaround (2-4 weeks)
- Fixed pricing (no surprises)
- Proven track record (1500+ users)
- IP ownership (full source code)
- Post-launch support (30 days)

### 5. **Conversion-Optimized CTA**
- "Book a free 15-min call" (not "Tell me your problem")
- US-friendly form fields (Email/Phone vs WhatsApp)
- Problem categories for US clients
- Clear response time commitment (<24hr)
